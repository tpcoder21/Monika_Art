About Monika Vishwakarma

Monika Vishwakarma is a certified and experienced Mehndi artist with over 3-4 years of expertise in creating stunning, traditional, and contemporary designs. Specializing in bridal, Arabic, and festival Mehndi, she brings elegance and artistry to every occasion, ensuring a personalized touch for her clients.
